About Our Village 💖

Khandawali is known for its peaceful environment, kind people, and beautiful landscapes. Surrounded by greenery, it offers a refreshing escape from city life. The village is home to traditions, festivals, and unity that reflect the essence of rural India.

Life here is simple yet fulfilling — from the vibrant festivals that light up every season, to the peaceful evenings where families gather under starlit skies.

Agriculture is the heartbeat of our village, with golden crops waving proudly in the fields, sustaining not just livelihoods but traditions.

What makes KHANDAWALI truly special is its spirit of togetherness.

Every visitor is welcomed like family, and every corner holds a memory worth cherishing. Whether it’s the serenity of our temples and ponds, our village is a timeless story waiting to be explored.

Festivals & Traditions 🎉

Holi

The festival of colors is celebrated with joy, dance, and community gatherings.

Diwali

The festival of lights brings families together with diyas, sweets, and celebrations.

Harvest Festival

Farmers thank nature for the crops with prayers, feasts, and cultural activities.

Local Fairs (Mela)

Khandawali organizes fairs with folk songs, traditional food, and handicrafts.
